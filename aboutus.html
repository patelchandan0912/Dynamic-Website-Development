About Us

Acho Hosting Solutions is a privately owned Web Hosting Service Provider (WHSP) with its data center located in Dearborn, MI USA.

Company Backround:

Acho Hosting Solutions (AHS) was founded December 19th 2003 by Nicholas R Acho. We currently provide web hosting services for customers in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom and Abroad. We specialize in shared and reseller hosting as well as domain names and SSL Certificates. As of April 1st 2006 AHS provides services for our customer base of 147+ customers. We offer competitive pricing and quality that will meet or exceed your web hosting standards. AHS COLO servers are located in Dearborn, Michigan at the Nexcess Data Center. Our present staff is made up of 8 members and is growing as our customer base continues to develop and expand. Acho Hosting Solutions - is a Sole Proprietorship and equal opportunity employer, for more information please contact legal@achohosting.com
