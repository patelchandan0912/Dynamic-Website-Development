Data Center

Acho Hosting Solutions is a privately owned Web Hosting Service Provider (WHSP) with its data center located in Dearborn, MI USA.

Racks and Edge Routine

Acho Hosting Solutions servers are mounted to custom designed server racks designed to maximize air flow and the efficiency of the data center cooling systems.

On the edge of the network, each server is connected directly to a Gbps capable layer 3 routing switch for maximum network flexibility and quality of service.

 

Geothermal Heat Pumps

Acho Hosting Solutions data center is cooled by redundant HVAC systems. These systems use 10 and 25 ton geothermal heat pump.

These pumps reduce the temperature inside the data center to an optimum 18 degrees centigrade.

Each unit is connected to UPS power and is continuously monitored.

 

Electronic Security Systems

Acho Hosting Solutions uses multi-layed electronic security systems to protect our data center as well as 24/7 onsite security.

This includes electronic entry, motion and breech alarms and a video surveillance system.

All entry to the data center is restricted to authorized personnel.

 

Redundant UPS Systems

Acho Hosting Solutions data center is filtered and conditioned by redundant UPS systems.

These UPS systems also carry the data center's power load during transition between our two primary hydroelectric feeds and between our utility feeds and our emergency diesel generators.

 

Diesel Back-Up Power Generators

Acho Hosting Solutions data center is connected to two separate hydroelectric utility grids:

Fortis and BCHydro.
In addition, it has standby diesel generators available to provide emergency power. These generators are tested weekly and can provide emergency power for an indefinite period of time.

 

Core Routing Equipment

Acho Hosting Solutions uses State-Of-The-Art networking equipment to route up and downstream traffic from our data center.

Redundant BGP4 border routers connect our data center to three different backbone carriers via OC-12 and Gig fiber links for maximum network reliability and performance.

 

Network Operations Center

Acho Hosting Solutions network operations center (NOC) is manned 24/7/365 by expert technicians to ensure maximum network reliability and performance.

From our NOC, network engineers can detect and resolve routing issues on our multi homes fiber network often before the end-users even notice the problem.
